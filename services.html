Our Services

Home / Our Services

Construction Machinery Supply

We supply a complete range of construction machinery including bar benders, bar cutters, concrete mixers, concrete vibrators, road rollers, power trowels, tamping rammers, and concrete groove cutters for all types of infrastructure projects.

Automatic Concrete Batching Plant

We supply fully automatic concrete batching plants designed for large-scale infrastructure projects. These plants ensure precise mix ratios, consistent concrete output, and faster production — ideal for road, bridge, and building contractors.

Expansion Joints

We provide high-quality expansion joints engineered for bridges, flyovers, and road surfaces. Designed to absorb structural movement and thermal expansion, our expansion joints ensure long-term durability and performance of infrastructure.

Lifting Material & Magnetic Lifters

Our lifting range includes chain pulley blocks, webbing slings, wire ropes, magnetic lifters, tower crane buckets, and material hoists — giving contractors everything needed for safe and efficient lifting operations on site.

Safety Products & Nets

Site safety is non-negotiable. We stock a complete PPE range including helmets, gloves, safety harnesses, high-visibility vests, rubber boots, goggles, safety nets, and traffic barriers — keeping your workforce protected at every stage.

Bitumen Products

We supply a comprehensive range of bitumen products through our partnership with Tiki Tar Donsa Pvt. Ltd. — including bitumen drums, road-grade bitumen, and surface treatment materials trusted across Maharashtra's road and highway projects.

Pipe Wrap & Corrosion Protection

We offer pipe wrap tapes and corrosion protection products from Tiki Tar Donsa Pvt. Ltd. — essential for protecting underground pipelines, water mains, and structural steel from moisture, rust, and chemical damage.

Tarpaulins & Curing Hessian Cloth

We provide heavy-duty tarpaulins and curing hessian cloth rolls essential for concrete curing, material storage, and weather protection. Available in multiple sizes to suit small residential sites as well as large infrastructure projects.

Centring Materials

We stock a full range of centring materials including shuttering plates, props, H-frames, and accessories — supporting formwork requirements for columns, slabs, beams, and other concrete structural elements.

Membrane & Water Stopper

Our waterproofing range includes bituminous membranes and PVC water stoppers for basements, underground structures, water-retaining tanks, and tunnels — providing a reliable barrier against water ingress and structural damage.

Garware Ropes & Technical Fibers

As authorised channel partners of Garware Technical Fibers Ltd., we supply industrial-grade ropes, safety nets, nylon nets, and technical fiber products used widely across construction, irrigation, bridge, and marine projects.

Survey & Civil Lab Equipment & Grease Pumps

From total stations and auto levels to cube moulds, compaction equipment, and testing sieves — we supply complete civil lab and survey instruments. We also stock grease pumps and maintenance tools to keep your machinery running at peak performance.
