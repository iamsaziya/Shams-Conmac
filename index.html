We supply a complete range of construction machinery including concrete mixers, bar benders, bar cutters, concrete vibrators, tamping rammers, road rollers, power trowels, groove cutters, generators, and automatic concrete batching plants — all available at our Aurangabad showroom.

Yes. We are authorised channel partners of Garware Technical Fibres Ltd., Esquire Construction Machine Pvt. Ltd., Kepro, and Tiki Tar Donsa Pvt. Ltd. Every product we sell is genuine and backed by the manufacturer.

We serve road contractors, railway contractors, bridge contractors, irrigation contractors, electrical contractors, and industrial buyers across Maharashtra. We also supply the agricultural sector for shade nets and tarpaulins.
