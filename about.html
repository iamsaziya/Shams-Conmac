More About Us

Over 55 Years of Legacy in Construction Supply

Shams Trading is one of Maharashtra's most trusted names in hardware and construction machinery supply. Founded with a simple promise — to bring contractors and infrastructure professionals the best materials at the right value — we have grown into a comprehensive one-stop supplier serving some of the region's most ambitious projects. Based in Aurangabad, our roots run deep in the construction industry. Across five decades, we have served irrigation contractors, railway contractors, electrical contractors, road builders, and bridge construction teams across Maharashtra and beyond.

What We Offer

A Complete Range of Construction Materials & Machinery

From heavy construction equipment to precision civil lab instruments, Shams Trading carries everything a serious contractor needs under one roof.

01

Construction Machinery

Bar benders, bar cutters, concrete mixers, concrete vibrators, road rollers, power trowels, concrete groove cutters, tamping rammers, and more — all from trusted brands.

02

Lifting & Material Handling

Chain pulley blocks, webbing slings, wire ropes, magnetic lifters, material hoists, tower crane buckets, and a full range of rigging accessories for safe lifting operations.

03

Expansion Joints & Bitumen Products

High-quality expansion joints for bridges and roads, along with bitumen products, pipe wraps, membranes, water stoppers, and Tiki Tar corrosion protection solutions.

04

Safety Products & Nets

Full PPE range including helmets, gloves, safety harnesses, high-visibility vests, safety nets, rubber boots, goggles, and site safety barriers.

05

Survey & Civil Lab Equipment

Total stations, levelling instruments, cube moulds, testing sieves, compaction equipment, and a comprehensive range of civil engineering laboratory tools.

06

Ropes, Tarpaulins & Curing Materials

Garware technical ropes and nets, heavy-duty tarpaulins, curing hessian cloth rolls, grease pumps, centring materials, and an extensive range of site essentials.
